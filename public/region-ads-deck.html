The Idea

One assembly line. Six regions.

Lee records each line once, for all six regions, as standalone clips. We mix the intro hook + the core message + a CTA. When a region is booked out, we turn the "openings now" lines off and the "booked out" line on — and shift ad spend to the regions that need demand.

The Lever

Same ad. Different dial.

Keep the structure identical across every region. When capacity shifts, swap "we've got openings now" for "we're usually booked out" and move the spend. One library of clips becomes a utilization control panel.

Light region
Turn availability lines ON. Increase spend. Push "openings now."
Booked region
Turn availability lines OFF. Run the waitlist ad. Reduce spend.
